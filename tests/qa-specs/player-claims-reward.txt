Feature: Player claims reward

A player must complete a task to unlock and claim a reward.

Setup:
- Create player with email <PLAYER_EMAIL>
- Create reward with name "Badge Unlock"
- Assign reward to player

Happy path:
- I am on the dashboard
- I click "Complete Task" button
- I should see "Task Completed" message
- I click "Claim Reward" button
- I should see "Badge Unlock" in my inventory

Task already completed:
- I am on the dashboard
- I should see "Task Completed" message
- I click "Claim Reward" button
- I should see "Already claimed" message

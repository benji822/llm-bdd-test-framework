Feature: Customer login

Users must authenticate with email and password to reach the dashboard.

Happy path:
- I am on the login page
- I enter email as <E2E_USER_EMAIL>
- I enter password as <E2E_USER_PASSWORD>
- I click the login button
- I should see text "Welcome back"

Invalid password:
- I am on the login page
- I enter email as <E2E_USER_EMAIL>
- I enter password as <E2E_INVALID_PASSWORD>
- I click the login button
- I should see text "Invalid credentials"
